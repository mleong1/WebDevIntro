Welcome to Matt's Burger Shack on Spruce Street, where good food and even better customer service meet for a luncheon with friends, catching up with family, or a romantic night out. We encourage anyone to try our house specialty burger made with our special, proprietary garlic aioli sauce, cheddar cheese, and topped with crisp lettuce and even crisper, slow-cooked bacon.
